# 📊 Swiggy Sales Analysis & Excel Dashboard

## 🧠 Executive Summary

This project presents an interactive Excel-based business dashboard analyzing Swiggy food delivery data. It provides insights into sales performance, customer ratings, order behavior, and regional trends to support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Food delivery platforms need to monitor:

* Revenue growth
* Customer satisfaction
* Order patterns
* Regional performance

👉 Key question:
How can sales, ratings, and order data be used to improve operational efficiency and customer experience?

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies

* Microsoft Excel
* Pivot Tables & Pivot Charts
* Slicers & Interactive Filters
* Data Cleaning & Transformation

---

## 📊 Key Metrics (KPIs)

* 💰 Total Sales: ₹53.01M
* ⭐ Average Rating: 4.34
* 🛒 Average Order Value: ₹268.51
* 📝 Rating Count: 5.59M
* 📦 Total Orders: 197.43K

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

* 📅 Monthly Sales Trend
* 📆 Daily Sales Trend
* 📈 Weekly Sales Trend
* 🍽 Sales by Food Category
* 🗺 Sales by State (India Map)
* 🏙 Top Cities by Sales
* 📊 Quarter-wise Sales, Ratings & Orders
* 🎛 Interactive Filters (Month, Category, Restaurant)

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

* 📈 Sales show consistent trends with periodic fluctuations across months
* 🍽 Certain food categories contribute significantly to total revenue
* 🗺 Major states dominate sales performance → regional concentration
* 🏙 Top cities generate a large share of revenue
* ⭐ High average rating (4.34) indicates strong customer satisfaction
* 📦 Order volume is consistently high → strong demand

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* 🎯 Focus marketing on top-performing cities and states
* 🍽 Promote high-performing food categories
* ⭐ Maintain service quality to sustain high ratings
* 📈 Optimize low-performing regions with targeted campaigns
* 🚀 Use weekly trends for demand forecasting and staffing
